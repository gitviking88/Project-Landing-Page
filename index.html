Copenhagen Motorcycle Club

This motorcycle club is located in the center of copenhagen

Our brotherhood consists of selveral elements

First of all, we are a motorcycle club. Our passion to work on our bikes and improve them is one of the key elements in being a member of CMC

As we are located in the center of Copenhagen, be prepared to ride the narrow but charming streets of this old city.

Part of being a member is also to contribute to the club's well-being. This can be done in many ways, as long as it benefits the club.
